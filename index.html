02 / Current work

Questions I am exploring

I am interested in methods that make AI systems reliable, interpretable, and useful in high stakes knowledge work.

Current work

Multimodal Evaluation of Scientific Documents

In an early stage literature review with Dr. Yue Feng at TU Darmstadt, I am investigating how multimodal AI can assess factual consistency and semantic alignment across scientific text, figures, tables, captions, and citations. My goal is to contribute to feedback that is reliable, explainable, and useful for scientific writing.
